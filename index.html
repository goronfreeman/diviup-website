Why DIVI UP?

DIVI UP is more than just an app on your phone, it's a way of life. It makes us mindful that all things are connected—that doing good is our ongoing social responsibility, not just that item you check off your to-do list. We want DIVI UP to remind you that community matters and comes first.

DIVI UP for Charities

Each time you pay to unlock a DIVI Deal on the mobile app, 50% is donated to charity. Why? Because DIVI donates HALF of our top-line revenue to our charitable partners.
